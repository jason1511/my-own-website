About Me

I’m an ICT graduate specialising in Software Technology, with a strong interest in web development and practical software solutions. I enjoy building systems that solve real-world problems, from small business management tools to interactive web applications.

I completed a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology (Software Technology) at Swinburne University of Technology, developing a solid foundation in programming, databases, networking, and web development through hands-on projects.

Outside of coursework, I’ve taken end-to-end ownership of real projects, including a desktop inventory and sales management application for a small electric bike retailer. I’ve also published mods on the Steam Workshop, which strengthened my experience working within existing systems and iterating based on user feedback.

Currently, I’m focused on strengthening my front-end skills with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and presenting my work clearly through a structured portfolio. I’m seeking a graduate or junior developer role where I can contribute to real projects while learning from an experienced team.
